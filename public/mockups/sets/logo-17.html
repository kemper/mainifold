Twisted vase · hex
A spiral vase whose horizontal cross-section is a hexagon, rotated a few degrees per layer as it rises — so crisp faceted ridges sweep up the form like geometric flutes. The primary mark prints itself: hex slices deposit onto the plate and stack upward, each one twisted from the one below, so the spiral visibly grows as the gently-bulged belly fills out and the neck closes in — a soft teal print-head glow riding the live layer. It tops out into the finished vase, holds, then re-runs (~4.8 s). The resting frame is the completed vase, so any still looks polished; favicon, monochrome and on-light show the finished faceted form, legible down to 16 px.
