Impossible cube
The classic impossible cube — a full isometric cube frame built from chunky square-section beams, two teal face-shades giving every beam real volume. One amber column threads straight down the center and carries the aha: it sits in front at the top, weaves behind the teal mid-beams, then re-emerges in front at the bottom — one straight bar that's simultaneously the cube's nearest and furthest edge. It reads as a machined 3D object the renderer almost printed, never a flat arrow or play-button, and the bold hexagonal silhouette holds together down to a 16 px favicon.
